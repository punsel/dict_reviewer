Units of Information

Units of Information (From Smallest to Largest)

Unit Symbol Equivalent Description
Bit b Base unit Smallest unit of data (0 or 1)
Nibble — 4 bits Half a byte
Byte B 8 bits Represents 1 character (e.g., 'A')
Kilobyte KB 1,024 Bytes Small text files
Megabyte MB 1,024 KB = 1,048,576 Bytes Photos, apps
Gigabyte GB 1,024 MB Videos, large apps
Terabyte TB 1,024 GB Hard drives, large storage
Petabyte PB 1,024 TB Data centers, cloud storage
Exabyte EB 1,024 PB Internet-scale storage
Zettabyte ZB 1,024 EB Entire global data scale
Yottabyte YB 1,024 ZB Beyond current real-world usage

Quick Summary (Conversion Chain)

1 Byte = 8 bits

1 KB = 1024 Bytes

1 MB = 1024 KB

1 GB = 1024 MB

1 TB = 1024 GB

1 PB = 1024 TB

1 EB = 1024 PB

1 ZB = 1024 EB

1 YB = 1024 ZB

1. How many bits are in 5 bytes?

Answer: 5 × 8 = 40 bits

2. Convert 2048 bytes to kilobytes (KB).

Answer: 2048 ÷ 1024 = 2 KB

3. How many bytes are in 4 kilobytes?

Answer: 4 × 1024 = 4096 bytes

4. 1 Megabyte (MB) = how many bytes?

Answer: 1 MB = 1,048,576 bytes

5. Convert 3 MB to KB.

Answer: 3 × 1024 = 3072 KB

6. Convert 5 GB to MB.

Answer: 5 × 1024 = 5120 MB

7. A file is 2.5 KB in size. How many bytes is it?

Answer: 2.5 × 1024 = 2560 bytes

8. How many KB are in 1 TB?

Answer: 1 TB = 1024 GB → 1024 × 1024 MB → 1024 × 1024 × 1024 KB = 1,073,741,824 KB

9. How many GB are in 2048 MB?

Answer: 2048 ÷ 1024 = 2 GB

10. Convert 56 bits to bytes.

Answer: 56 ÷ 8 = 7 bytes

11. A system has 16 GB of RAM. How many MB is this?

Answer: 16 × 1024 = 16384 MB

12. 1 Nibble = how many bits?

Answer: 4 bits

13. Convert 3 TB to GB.

Answer: 3 × 1024 = 3072 GB

14. A flash drive is 32 GB. How many MB is that?

Answer: 32 × 1024 = 32768 MB

15. Convert 7 MB to bytes.

Answer: 7 × 1,048,576 = 7,340,032 bytes

16. How many TB are in 10,240 GB?

Answer: 10,240 ÷ 1024 = 10 TB

17. Convert 512 bytes to bits.

Answer: 512 × 8 = 4096 bits

18. A movie file is 2.4 GB. Convert to MB.

Answer: 2.4 × 1024 = 2457.6 MB

19. Convert 1 PB to TB.

Answer: 1 PB = 1024 TB

20. Convert 1 EB to GB.

Answer: 1 EB = 1024 PB → 1024 × 1024 TB → 1024 × 1024 × 1024 GB = 1,073,741,824 GB
