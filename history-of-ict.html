History of ICT

Abacus

Year: ~3000 BC

Contribution/Importance: First known calculating tool used for arithmetic.

Blaise Pascal

Year: 1642

Contribution/Importance: Invented Pascaline, the first mechanical calculator.

Gottfried Wilhelm Leibniz

Year: 1673

Contribution/Importance: Improved mechanical calculator; introduced binary system.

Joseph Marie Jacquard

Year: 1804

Contribution/Importance: Invented punch cards for automated weaving → influenced computers.

Charles Babbage

Year: 1837

Contribution/Importance: Designed Analytical Engine → considered Father of the Computer.

Ada Lovelace

Year: 1843

Contribution/Importance: Wrote first algorithm; First Computer Programmer.

Samuel Morse

Year: 1844

Contribution/Importance: Invented telegraph and Morse code.

Alexander Graham Bell

Year: 1876

Contribution/Importance: Invented the telephone.

Herman Hollerith

Year: 1890

Contribution/Importance: Invented tabulating machine & punch cards for census → founded IBM.

Guglielmo Marconi

Year: 1895

Contribution/Importance: Invented wireless radio communication.

John Ambrose Fleming

Year: 1904

Contribution/Importance: Invented vacuum tube (first electronic computer component).

Lee de Forest

Year: 1906

Contribution/Importance: Invented triode vacuum tube → amplified signals.

Konrad Zuse

Year: 1941

Contribution/Importance: Built Z3, the first working programmable computer.

Alan Turing

Year: 1943

Contribution/Importance: Broke Nazi code; proposed Turing Machine → Father of Computer Science.

John von Neumann

Year: 1945

Contribution/Importance: Designed computer architecture where data and program share memory.

ENIAC

Year: 1946

Contribution/Importance: First general-purpose electronic computer; used vacuum tubes.

Transistor Invented (Bell Labs)

Year: 1947

Contribution/Importance: Replaced vacuum tubes; led to faster, smaller computers.

UNIVAC I

Year: 1951

Contribution/Importance: First commercially sold computer.

Integrated Circuit (IC) – Jack Kilby

Year: 1958

Contribution/Importance: Allowed multiple transistors on a chip; led to microcomputers.

ARPANET

Year: 1969

Contribution/Importance: First internet network created; backbone of modern Internet.

Intel 4004 Microprocessor

Year: 1971

Contribution/Importance: First commercial microprocessor (4-bit).

Email Invented – Ray Tomlinson

Year: 1971

Contribution/Importance: Sent the first email; introduced @ symbol.

Apple Founded by Jobs & Wozniak

Year: 1976

Contribution/Importance: Popularized personal computers (Apple I, Apple II).

IBM PC Released

Year: 1981

Contribution/Importance: Standardized personal computing.

CD-ROM Introduced

Year: 1982

Contribution/Importance: Enabled digital data storage and multimedia.

World Wide Web – Tim Berners-Lee

Year: 1989

Contribution/Importance: Invented WWW, HTTP & HTML → Father of the Web.

Linux – Linus Torvalds

Year: 1991

Contribution/Importance: Created open-source Linux OS.

Wi-Fi Standard Introduced

Year: 1997

Contribution/Importance: Enabled wireless communication across networks.

Google Founded (Page & Brin)

Year: 1998

Contribution/Importance: Revolutionized search engines and cloud services.

Smartphone Revolution – iPhone

Year: 2007

Contribution/Importance: Started modern mobile computing era.
