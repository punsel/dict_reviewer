Number System

1. Convert 101101₂ to decimal.

1×2⁵ + 0×2⁴ + 1×2³ + 1×2² + 0×2¹ + 1×2⁰ = 32 + 8 + 4 + 1 = 45

Answer: 45₁₀

2. Convert 57₁₀ to binary.

Divide by 2 repeatedly → 111001₂

Answer: 111001₂

3. Convert 3A₁₆ to decimal.

3×16 + 10 = 48 + 10 = 58

Answer: 58₁₀

4. Convert 125₈ to binary.

1→001, 2→010, 5→101 → 001010101 → 1010101₂

Answer: 1010101₂

5. Convert 100101₂ to hexadecimal.

Groups: 0010 0101 = 2 5 → 25₁₆

Answer: 25₁₆

6. Convert 250₁₀ to hexadecimal.

250 ÷ 16 = 15 rem 10 → F A → FA₁₆

Answer: FA₁₆

7. Convert 1101010₂ to octal.

1 101 010 → 001 101 010 → 1 5 2 → 152₈

Answer: 152₈

  10101

 100011

Answer: 100011₂

-01010

 10001

Answer: 10001₂

10. Multiply 101₂ × 11₂

5 × 3 = 15 → 1111₂

Answer: 1111₂

11. Convert -13₁₀ to 8-bit two’s complement.

13 = 00001101; Invert → 11110010; Add 1 → 11110011

Answer: 11110011₂

12. Convert -5₁₀ to 8-bit two’s complement.

5 = 00000101 → Invert → 11111010 → +1 → 11111011

Answer: 11111011₂

13. Interpret 11110101₂ as a signed 8-bit number.

Starts with 1 → negative. Invert: 00001010 → +1 → 00001011 = 11. So = -11

Answer: -11₁₀

14. Convert 7F₁₆ to binary.

7 → 0111, F → 1111 → 01111111₂

Answer: 01111111₂

15. Convert 10101101₂ to decimal.

128+32+8+4+1=173

Answer: 173₁₀

16. Convert 255₁₀ to hex.

255 ÷ 16 → F rem F → FF₁₆

Answer: FF₁₆

17. Convert 101100₂ to octal.

Groups: 101 100 → 5 4 → 54₈

Answer: 54₈

18. Convert 432₈ to decimal.

4×64 + 3×8 + 2×1 = 256 + 24 + 2 = 282

Answer: 282₁₀

19. Add in hex: 3A₁₆ + 2F₁₆

3A=58, 2F=47 → 58+47=105 → 69₁₆

Answer: 69₁₆

20. Convert 10000000₂ (8-bit) to decimal signed.

Leading 1 → negative. Invert → 01111111 → +1 → 10000000 = 128 → So = -128

Answer: -128₁₀
